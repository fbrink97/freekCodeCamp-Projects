Lord of The Rings Facts

Written by J.R.R Tolkien, published 29th July 1954.

Movie and Book Facts

  • LoTR takes place at the end of the Third Age.
  • Tolkien wrote at least 3 languages for the books.
  • The Rohirrim during the battle of Minis Tirith is made up of women.
  • Peter Jackson allowed the Mauri of New Zealand to be a part of the films.
  • They made use of camera angles instead of special effects to show the height difference between the hobbits and the rest of the races.
